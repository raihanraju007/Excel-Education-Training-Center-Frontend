Alternative Test

NAT-TEST

The Japanese Language NAT-TEST is an examination that measures Japanese language ability of non-native speakers. Five levels (1-5) match the JLPT N1-N5 scale, and the format and types of questions are equivalent — making it an excellent alternative test held more frequently throughout the year.
