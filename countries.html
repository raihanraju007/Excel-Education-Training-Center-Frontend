Japan as a Destination
for Higher Education

Japan has one of the most influential positions in global business and technology. Japanese-language education creates employment opportunities with multinational companies around the world.

Japan is currently at the top of the list for students seeking higher education worldwide. Considering Japan's advanced education system, learning environment, culture and climate — students' interest will continue to grow.

Various scholarships and tuition discounts are available — under government, NGO and university scholarship schemes, students can continue studies completely free of cost.

United Kingdom — Worldwide Recognition

The UK has become one of the top destinations to study around the world. Traditions of excellence dating back hundreds of years, high-class institutions on every corner, and far more flexibility than many other countries.

The UK is the second-most popular destination for international students after the US. EETC partners with several London-based and regional UK institutions for student admissions.

Australia — Heart of Diversity

Australia ranks among the best study destinations in the world — competing aggressively with the US and the UK and winning on many fronts.

Graduates of Australian universities are highly sought after by employers worldwide for their practical training and high level of knowledge. Just as EETC offers a holistic approach to education, this culture is replicated across Australian higher education institutions.

The country has embraced cultural, political and religious diversity — international students attest to the friendly nature of Australian people.

Vocational Training

Unique opportunity to develop technical skills for direct workplace entry.

Cultural Diversity

People from all corners of the world live in Australia.

Lower Cost

Cost of living lower than other top destinations like the US and UK.

Work While Study

Students can work while studying — covers cost of living.

Post-Study Work

Australian rules allow remaining and gaining work experience after graduation.

Holistic Education

Vocational, technical and academic training combined.
