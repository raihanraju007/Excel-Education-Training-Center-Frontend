Pre-IELTS Preparatory Course

Pre-IELTS is a course specially developed by EETC for students who need to take the IELTS exam but whose level of English is too low to achieve a desirable score or to join a standard exam preparation course. It is an intensive 10–15 week course only available at EETC.

IELTS students will have four hours of classes per day, including a period of guided self-study.

Course Coverage

  • Understanding the IELTS exam sections
  • Pondering on and analyzing common IELTS topics
  • Developing study skills with time challenge
  • Improving academic and discreet vocabulary
  • Understanding question prompts
  • Practicing note taking
IELTS Skills

Four Skills, Equal Weight

Reading

Comprehension across articles, journals and academic texts.

Writing

Task 1 graphs and Task 2 essay structuring with academic tone.

Listening

Practice with native speakers, accents and varied speeds.

Speaking

Mock interviews and fluency drills for all three IELTS speaking parts.
