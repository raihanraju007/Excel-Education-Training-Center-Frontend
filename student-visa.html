Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

Language School

12+ years of education, basic Japanese (N5+ recommended).

Vocational College

12 years of education, JLPT N2 or equivalent Japanese skills.

Undergraduate

12 years of education, JLPT N2 + EJU or English-track program.

Postgraduate

Bachelor's degree, IELTS / N1 / N2 depending on program.

Documents

Document Checklist

Get all of these ready and your visa journey is half-done.

Valid passport (min 1 year)
National ID / Birth certificate
Academic certificates & transcripts
JLPT / NAT-TEST certificate
Bank statement (sponsor)

Recent passport photographs

Process

Step-by-Step Process

1. Free Counseling

Discuss your goals and academic background with our counselors.

2. School Selection

We shortlist 2-3 partner schools matching your budget and goal.

3. Application Submission

Documents prepared and submitted to the school for COE.

4. COE Issuance

Certificate of Eligibility issued by Japanese immigration (60–90 days).

5. Tuition Payment

First-year tuition transferred to the partner school.

6. Visa Stamping

Embassy submission and visa stamp on your passport.

7. Pre-Departure

Briefing on accommodation, airport pickup and arrival logistics.
