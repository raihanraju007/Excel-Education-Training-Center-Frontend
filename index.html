Improving Lives Through Academic Excellence

Excel Education & Training Center (EETC) started in 2015 to improve lives through quality education. Since 2017 we have grown into a complete Japan-route agency — language, visas and placement under one roof.

We have placed 11 engineers in Japanese organizations and 100+ students in language schools, colleges and universities with a 98% visa approval rate.
